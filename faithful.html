Faithful Coach Watch

A side-project one page Rails app that utilizes the Twitter API to display the last 100 tweets with the words, "49ers" and "Coach." Made and deployed in one day, this was more of a fun pet project that allowed me to get a feel for the Twitter gem.

As an added bonus, there is also a "Jed York" watch page that brings up any mentions whatsoever of York on Twitter. Things are pretty toxic these days, in regards to the 49ers ownership, and sometimes it's a lot of fun to sit back and read the vitriol. Enjoy!

Currently deployed at: http://faithfulcoachwatch.herokuapp.com/
